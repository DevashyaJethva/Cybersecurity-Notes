#  UDP (User Datagram Protocol)

UDP is a Transport layer protocol designed for speed.

Unlike TCP, UDP is stateless and connectionless.

There is:

- no handshake
- no acknowledgements
- no guaranteed delivery
- no retransmission

---

#  Characteristics

- connectionless communication
- minimal overhead
- fast delivery

---

# Advantages
- faster than TCP
- no connection setup
- lower processing load

#  Disadvantages
- no delivery guarantee
- no error correction
- packets may arrive out of order

---

#  Where UDP is Used

UDP is used when speed matters more than accuracy:

- video streaming
- online gaming
- VoIP calls
- live broadcasting
